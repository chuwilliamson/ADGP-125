Sister of Captain Britain, Betsy Braddock is the psychic X-Man known as Psylocke. Originally just a telepath and telekinetic, Betsy ended up switching bodies with a deadly assassin that allowed her to become the psychic ninja she is today. Psylocke uses her telekinetic powers to manifest a "Psi-Knife" and her trained ninja skills to slip into the shadows and cut enemies down with her martial arts and katana skills.
